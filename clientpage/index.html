Personal Transformation Matrix

This is a vocal slicer that you control by twisting the parameters using pinch gestures. Pinch with your left hand to decrease the values, pinch with your right hand to increase the values.

Load a sample to begin

Keyboard shortcuts

RShow or hide sample panel (starts playback when you close it)
MMute / unmute all audio
GToggle grid lines
ZReset grid rotations
VToggle video cover / contain mode
DToggle debug overlay (parameter values)
HShow or hide this help

Left-hand pinch decreases values; right-hand pinch increases. Press H or Esc to close help.
